Overview

Everyday Moments, Brought to Life
In collaboration with Google Southeast Asia and Google Indonesia, we used Gemini’s image-to-video tool and Veo3 to explore a new way of visual storytelling.

People uploaded their own images snapshots of daily life, culture, and personal moments. Using a simple prompt structure, these photos were turned into short, moving videos filled with subtle motion and emotion.

The videos were shared across Google Indonesia’s social channels, celebrating real stories from real people  brought to life through thoughtful technology.
