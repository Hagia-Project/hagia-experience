Overview

On 5 Nov 2025—Hari Cinta Puspa & Satwa Nasional—we brought six Google Doodles to life from google.com and on the giant screen at #Chase Plaza, #Jakarta using #Veo3.1 , to spotlight endangered species.



G O O G L E — spelled by the animals:

G — Whale Shark

O — Orangutan

O — Rafflesia

G — Cenderawasih (Bird-of-paradise)

L — Corpse Flower (Amorphophallus titanum)

E — Manta Ray
