AI Video Generation



CLIENT   Google

LOCATION   Indonesia

PARTNER   -

YEAR   2025



The Google Billboard Project was created to transform Indonesia’s most iconic screens into a living, breathing showcase of creativity. Using Veo’s next-generation AI video technology, we turned real-time cultural trends and viral moments into dynamic billboard experiences across the country.

Students and young creators contributed their own ideas and visuals, which were adapted and brought to life on the billboards turning the streets into an open-air gallery of human creativity and local expression.

The result was a living advertising house, where technology and imagination merged to reflect the pulse of Indonesia’s ever-evolving culture.
